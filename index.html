ATS Homekraft Pious Orchard

ATS Homekraft Pious Orchard (RERA No.:- UPRERAPRJ183246) is the new project launched during Navratri 2021 which is adjacent to our existing project ATS Homekraft Pious Hideaways. Basically, Pious Orchard can be called as the 2nd phase of Pious Hideaways too, but both have separate RERA approvals and UP-RERA registration numbers. Both the phases (ATS Homekraft Pious Orchard & ATS Homekraft Pious Hideaways) are spread across 18 acres of the land parcels if combined together. ATS Homekraft Pious Hideaways spread across 8 acres (approx) & ATS Homekraft Pious Orchard is spread across 10 acres (approx).



If we talk about the unit configurations in ATS Homekraft Pious Orchard, there will be only 2 types of units: 2350 sq.ft. (3 BHK + Study Room + Servant Room) & 3200 sq.ft. (5 BHK + Servant Room).ATS Homekraft Pious Orchard will have only 608 flats in total across 10 towers. There will be 5 towers of 2350 sq.ft. configuration (only 2 units/floor) and 5 towers of 3200 sq.ft. configuration (it also has only 2 units/floor). It means, 304 units each of 2350 sq.ft. & 3200 sq. ft. Tower height would be Stilt + 31 floors & Stilt + 32 floors. So, ATS Homekraft Pious Orchard has comparatively bigger unit layouts than ATS Homekraft Pious Hideaways (which had 1400 sq.ft., 1600 sq.ft., and 1675 sq.ft. layouts).



ATS Homekraft Pious Orchard is also one of the most spacious & greenest projects of Sector-150 Noida, with super-premium amenities. It has a vast central green park of its own and also overlooking the adjoining central park of ATS Homekraft Pious Hideaways. So, if we virtually combine both the central parks, it will look massive from every park facing balconies of both the projects. And that is one of its biggest USPs as well. Whereas on the backside of ATS Homekraft Pious Orchard, balconies will be overlooking the endless views of the Yamuna river crossing through the huge green land bank. ATS Homekraft Pious Orchard, when delivered, will surely be a project to reckon and feel proud of.
